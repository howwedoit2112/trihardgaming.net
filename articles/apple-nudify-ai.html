Apple and Google Ordered to Take Down AI "Nudify" Apps

Emma Roth Jul 17, 2026 Source: The Verge

San Francisco city attorney David Chiu sent cease-and-desist letters to both companies, demanding that they remove 13 apps that can be used to generate nude images of someone without their consent.

The letters ask Google and Apple to stop "aiding and abetting" the sale of sexualized AI deepfakes, which is prohibited under California law.

What Happened

  • 13 apps total: 8 on Apple, 5 on Android
  • Google: Suspended the 5 Android apps cited in the letter
  • Apple: Removed 3 apps and terminated developer accounts

"We have always strictly prohibited apps designed to generate, distribute, or consume pornography. 'Nudification' apps are against our App Review Guidelines."

— Adam Dema, Apple spokesperson
