Sony's Flagship RGB LED TV Is Incredible

John Higgins Jul 18, 2026 Source: The Verge

Sony's Bravia 9 II is the latest flagship in the Japanese tech giant's TV lineup. The RGB LED panel delivers stunning color accuracy and contrast that rivals OLED displays.

Review Highlights

  • RGB LED tech: Full-color LED backlighting for precise color reproduction
  • Gaming features: 120Hz refresh rate, VRR, and ultra-low input lag
  • Smart TV: Google TV with full app support

The Verge's review gave the Bravia 9 II strong marks across the board, calling it "incredible" for both movie watching and gaming.
