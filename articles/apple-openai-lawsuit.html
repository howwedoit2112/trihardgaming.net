Apple's Lawsuit Against OpenAI: The 6 Wildest Claims

Emma Roth Jul 17, 2026 Source: The Verge

Apple filed a lawsuit against OpenAI last week, accusing the AI giant of stealing trade secrets. The 41-page complaint details how OpenAI allegedly "coached" Apple employees on how to avoid security checks and asked for "show and tell" during job interviews.

The Lawsuit Breakdown

  • Trade secrets: OpenAI employees were coached to memorize data before leaving Apple
  • 40 employees targeted: Apple sent legal warning letters to dozens of former employees
  • 6 key claims: Including stolen data, coached employees, and hidden training methods
