The PS6 Sure Sounds Like a Handheld

Sean Hollister Jul 15, 2026 Source: The Verge

Sony's next PlayStation console sounds like it's going to be a handheld device, based on recent business meetings about the future of gaming.

What We Know

  • Handheld focus: Sony is prioritizing portable gaming for the next generation
  • End of discs: Physical media is fading as games go digital-first
  • PC business growth: Sony is expanding into PC gaming alongside console

The Future of Physical Games

The future of physical games isn't looking great. With GTA VI and other blockbusters going digital-first, the days of buying boxed games are numbered. Sony's push toward handheld suggests the PlayStation of the future fits in your bag, not under your TV.
